JovTech Workshop: Design & Code for the Future

I hosted the Jov Tech Workshop on October 26, 2026, at SM Calamba, where participants learned the fundamentals of design and programming. About JovTech

About Me

I’m Joverich Lelis, a 21-year-old student from Calamba City, Laguna, who’s passionate about technology, creativity, and personal growth. Ever since I discovered the world of programming and design, I’ve been fascinated by how imagination and logic come together to create something meaningful. For me, technology isn’t just a tool it’s a way to express ideas, solve problems, and build something that can inspire others.

I love exploring new things whether it’s coding a system, designing visuals, or learning new digital tools. Each project I work on teaches me something valuable about persistence and creativity. My biggest goal is to become a professional software developer and use my skills to bring innovation into people’s lives. I believe that through technology, we can make a real difference and turn even the simplest ideas into reality.

Outside of coding, I enjoy photography, editing, and discovering creative ways to share stories through visuals. I find inspiration in everyday experiences and in people who are driven by passion and purpose.
